Welcome to Aaryans TMUA Preparation Website

This website helps students prepare for the TMUA examination. Disclaimer: A thorough knowledge of AS level maths is expected at a minimum. This includes the understanding. In addition to this the TMUA exam does contain topics from year 2 maths (A-level) so whilst these topics will be covered it is adviseable to learn them aswel in greater depth.
